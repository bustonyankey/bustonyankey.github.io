These are fresh miracle berry fruits that have water taken out of the fruits through the process of freeze drying. The technology used to dry the berry allows the shape, colour and all the essential nutrients to be retained. Unlike the fresh or frozen miracle berry, the dehydrated miracle berry is much more stable and has longer storage life when stored in air tight package.

The dehydrated miracle berry fruit also contains the same composition (rich vitamins antioxidants and the glycoprotein called miraculin) as that of the fresh miracle berry fruit, except water that has been removed through freeze drying. When the dehydrated miracle berry fruit (two fruits) is consumed, the miraculin in the dehydrated fruits binds to the taste buds on the tongue and that causes the sweet receptors on their taste buds to identify sour food or drink as sweet. For example; a lemon suddenly tastes like lemonade and vinegar tastes like apple juice. For up to an hour all acidic and sour flavors will be masked and everything consumed will taste sweet.

The difference between the miracle berry fruit powder and the dehydrated miracle berry fruit is the presence of the seed in the dehydrated miracle berry fruit.

USES/HEALTH BENEFITS

  • It helps in removing the metallic taste associated with chemotherapy patients.
  • It serves as a natural taste enhancer for Diabetic patients.
  • It serves as a Food flavor enhancer for dieters.
  • It helps reduce sugar intake and thus controlling body weight and tooth decay.
  • It contains Anti-oxidant properties and active polyphenols needed for healthy growth.

DOSAGE/ DIRECTION FOR USE

  • Put at least 2-3 dehydrated miracle berry fruits in your mouth (depending on the sensitivity of your taste buds) Gently use your teeth and tongue to remove the seed from the rest of the fruit.
  • Swirl the pulp on the seed around the tongue to coat the tongue. Do not chew the seed (since is slightly bitter).
  • Do not worry if you mistakenly crack the seed, because it is also safe. But the seed is bitter, so gently use your teeth and tongue to separate the seed from fruit. And ensure that the pulp effective coat the tongue.
  • Once the tongue is effectively coated with the fruit pulp, The tongue is now modified to perceive sour or acidic food and drink as sweet.

HANDLING/ STORAGE CONDITIONS
Always keep the dehydrated miracle berry fruit in an air-tight bag or container and store at a temperature of 10oC. Do not expose the dehydrated miracle berry fruits to air, moisture and high temperature (above 20oC) during storage


SHIPMENT CONDITIONS
The dehydrated miracle berry fruit is shipped under ambient conditions in vacuum sealed cellophane bags (which are air tight). Shipments are done usually by air through courier services.
