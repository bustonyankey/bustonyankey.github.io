The WondaTaste dried fruits also contains the same composition (rich vitamins, antioxidants and the glycoprotein called miraculin) as that of the fresh miracle berry fruit, except water that has been removed through freeze drying. When only one freeze-dried miracle fruit is consumed, the miraculin in the dried fruits binds to the taste buds on the tongue and that causes the sweet receptors on their taste buds to identify sour food or drink as sweet. For example, a lemon suddenly tastes like lemonade and vinegar tastes like apple juice. For up to an hour all acidic and sour flavours will be masked, and everything consumed will taste sweet.

We have two main types of the dried miracle fruits available: The whole dried miracle fruits and the seedless or halved dried miracle fruit.


Seedless Dried Miracle Berries
These are whole dried miracle fruits that are cut into two equal halves and the seeds removed from the fruits. This provides the convenience of not having anything to deal with the crushing of the bitter seeds. The freeze-dried seedless fruits also have the original taste, colour and nutritional composition as the fresh miracle fruits.


PRODUCT ADVANTAGE/ QUALITY
We ensure that only fresh and high-quality miracle fruits are freeze-dried (to have water taken out of the fruits). The berries are 100% natural and they are handpicked from our organic farms. The freeze-drying technology used to dry the berry allows the taste, shape, colour and all the essential nutrients in the fruits to be retained. Unlike the fresh or frozen miracle berry fruits, the WondaTaste dried berries are much more stable (prolong shelf life) when stored in an air-tight package.
