PEJ Grain Mix is a special whole grain meal made from a mixture of whole grains which combines the nutritional benefit of cereals and legumes.


PRODUCT ADVANTAGE/ QUALITY
The PEJ Grain Mix is produced from 100% natural, high quality whole grains free from insects and debris to give a wholesome grain mix.

USES/HEALTH BENEFITS

  • It is high in fiber that helps maintain free bowels and relief consumer from constipation.
  • The soyabeans and wheat are rich in protein, which helps to supply the protein needs of the consumer.
  • The brown rice is rich in carbohydrate, which helps to supply the carbohydrate needs of the consumer.

DOSAGE/ DIRECTION FOR USE

  • Mix a satisfactory quantity of PEJ Grain-Mix with water.
  • Put the mixture on fire and stir continuously till it boils.
  • Allow it to boil for 3-5 minutes more (it thickens when it boils, so add appropriate quantity of water to get the desired thickness.
  • Serve with milk and sugar if desired.

HANDLING/ STORAGE CONDITIONS
The PEJ Grain Mix must be kept in an air-tight container and stored under a cool dry place. Do not expose the PEJ Grain Mix to moisture during storage.
